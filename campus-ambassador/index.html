Campus Ambassador Program

The Campus Ambassador program is SketchOff's elite strike team for user growth. They are stationed at colleges all over the U.S. and build SketchOff on their campuses. They are given power to devise, launch, and monitor their own local media campaigns through which they learn how to create their own peer-to-peer buzz and increase adoption for a new and revolutionary product. They track the results of their own efforts, continually modify their strategies, and evolve into marketing gurus.

Want in? Send your résumé to contact@sketch-off.com, and we'll talk.
